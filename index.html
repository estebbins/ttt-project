Tic-Tac-Toe

Instructions: decide who will be player X and player O. Take turns selecting squares. Adjust the first player by clicking on "Change First" button. Until you refresh or leave the page, or Reset Statistics, your win-records will be recorded. Restart the current game at any time by clicking "New Game". Once a player wins or the game has resulted in a tie, the score will be recorded and you can start a New Game!
