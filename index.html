Tic-Tac-Toe

Instructions: Decide who will be player 1 and player 2. You can change which player goes first by clicking on "Change First" button. Until you refresh or leave the page, or reset the gameboard, your records will display! Click "New Game" at any time to reset the current game. Once a player wins or the game has resulted in a tie, the "New Game Button" will turn green and your score will be recorded.
